Hero Dogs

Hero dogs exemplify the extraordinary capabilities of everyday dogs, often stepping up to save or protect humans and other animals, sometimes at great personal risk.

Golden Toby

Toby saved his owner from choking by performing a makeshift Heimlich maneuver, jumping on her chest until the food was dislodged.

Source: Reisen, 2021. American Kennel Club

Major the Hero

Major, a Pit/Labrador mix, called emergency services during his owner's seizure, ensuring medical help arrived in time.

Working Dogs

Working dogs are specially trained to perform essential tasks that support humans across various environments, from guarding to search and rescue.

Barry the Saint Bernard

Barry saved over 40 lives as a rescue dog in the Swiss Alps. Known for his strength and courage, he remains a symbol of rescue work.

Source: Fetch Pet Care, n.d. Fetch Pet Care

Nemo the German Shepherd

Nemo, a U.S. Air Force dog, saved his handler’s life during combat, continuing to protect him even after being wounded.

Companion Dogs

Companion dogs play a vital role in enhancing the emotional well-being of their human partners, offering support, comfort, and companionship.

Paris the Ecofriendly Boxer

Paris helps the environment by instinctively collecting cans and plastic bottles during her walks and placing them in recycling bins.

Source: Reisen, 2021. American Kennel Club
