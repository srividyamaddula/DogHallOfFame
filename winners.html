Winners of the Hall of Fame

Toby, a Golden Retriever, saved his owner Debbie Parkhurst during a choking incident. Using his paws, he pushed Debbie to the floor and performed a makeshift Heimlich maneuver, successfully dislodging the apple from her throat. His quick thinking and loyalty made him a true hero.

Source: Reisen, 2021. American Kennel Club

Did You Know?

Golden Retrievers are ranked among the top 10 most intelligent dog breeds in the world.

Nemo, a German Shepherd, served in the U.S. Air Force in South Vietnam. During an enemy attack, Nemo alerted his handler, saving lives. Despite severe injuries, Nemo displayed unmatched bravery, solidifying his place as a legendary working dog.

Source: Dugal, 2024. Dogster

Interesting Fact:

German Shepherds are widely used as military and police dogs due to their intelligence and loyalty.

Frankie, a Golden Retriever, brought joy to nursing home residents, especially Jean, who found comfort in Frankie’s presence. Through weekly visits, Frankie helped Jean engage with others and find happiness in her final days.

Source: Canidae, n.d. Canidae

Fun Fact:

Therapy dogs like Frankie reduce stress, anxiety, and depression in humans.
